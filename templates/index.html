Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Train Delay Risk Prediction system work? ▾
Select the origin station, destination station, journey date, and train type. Click "Predict Delay Risk" and the system analyzes historical railway patterns to estimate the delay probability.
What does the Delay Risk Percentage indicate? ▾
It represents the likelihood that the selected train may be delayed. Low risk, Moderate risk, High risk based on historical delay patterns.
Which machine learning model is used? ▾
Both Decision Tree and Random Forest models were tested. Random Forest produced more stable and accurate results, so it is used for the final delay risk prediction.
How accurate are these predictions? ▾
The model learns delay patterns from historical railway operations and predicts future delay probability. Reliability depends on how similar future conditions are to past patterns.
Which factors and data influence the delay prediction? ▾
Weather conditions and seasonal patterns are key influences. The system also analyzes route combinations, weekday trends, train type, and operational railway data to identify delay patterns.
